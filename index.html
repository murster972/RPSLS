What is this?

This is an implementation of the game "rock, paper, scissors, lizard, spock", a variation of the original game popularized by its appereance in an episode of the big-bang theory.

Rock crushes Scissors

How to Play

To play click either of the buttons at the top. There are two game-modes avaible, Normal and Arcade.

In normal mode the user selects their piece and then the opponents piece is randomly generated and the results shown.

In arcade mode random pieces are generated and pop-up on the screen, the player must use the keys (1-5) to select a piece to defeat the opponent; every time they select a correct piece the score is increased, but the timer also gets faster. The game ends when the player guesses an incorrect piece or the timer runs out.
